FAQ
Common questions about using the Birdie Board.
📲 Installing the App
How do I install on iPhone? ▼
  1. Open Safari and go to fairwayhq.github.io/CCCC--BB
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow)
  3. Tap Add to Home Screen → Add
How do I install on Android? ▼
  1. Open Chrome and go to fairwayhq.github.io/CCCC--BB
  2. Tap ⋮ → Add to Home Screen or Install App
  3. Tap Add
🔔 Notifications
How do I enable notifications on iPhone? ▼

Important: On iPhone, push notifications only work if the app is installed to your home screen. iOS 16.4+ required.

  1. Install the app to your home screen first
  2. Open the app from the home screen icon
  3. When prompted, tap Allow
  4. If no prompt: go to Settings → Notifications → Birdie Board
How do I enable notifications on Android? ▼

In Chrome browser: When prompted tap Allow. If missed: tap lock icon → Notifications → Allow.

Installed app: Open from home screen, tap Allow. If on Android 13+: go to Settings → Apps → Birdie Board → Notifications.

Battery optimization: Go to Settings → Apps → Birdie Board → Battery → select Unrestricted.

What triggers a notification? ▼
  • Someone posts a new score
  • Someone comments on a score
  • Someone reacts to a score
  • A member completes the card
🔑 Account & PIN
I forgot my PIN — how do I reset it? ▼
  1. Tap Sign In in the top nav
  2. Tap Forgot your PIN?
  3. Select your name and choose a new PIN
  4. Tap Request PIN Reset
  5. Wait for admin approval
Will I stay signed in? ▼

Yes — the app remembers you automatically. You only need to sign in again if you tap Sign Out or clear your browser data.

⛳ Scoring
What are the point values? ▼
  • 🐦 Birdie — 1pt (counts toward Birdie Race)
  • 🦅 Eagle — 3pts
  • 🦉 Albatross / Double Eagle — 4pts
  • 🎯 Hole-in-One — 5pts
What is the Birdie Race? ▼

The Birdie Race tracks who is first to birdie all 18 holes. Only birdies count — not eagles or aces. First to complete all 18 earns the Wall of Fame.
